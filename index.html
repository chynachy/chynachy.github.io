Etsy Seller CX

Handmade jewelry is one of my favorite things to get from Etsy. I recently purchased a yogi mala necklace made of tigers eye beads and a beautiful brass charm sourced from Ethiopia.
The seller's note promised shipping within 2 business days, but I had seen no update for 4 days. Despite feeling frustrated and impatient, I decided not to cancel the order. Rather than
writing to ask about the hold up from a place of impatience, I simply chose to give it another day or two before I requested a refund. To my delight, the seller wrote to me shortly after
informing me that my necklace had been shipped to arrive the next day. They paid to rush the delivery without me having to ask. My neklace was beautifully packaged with a handwritten card
thanking me for my patience and business. In addition to that, they sent me a free pair of earrings and a discount code for my next order. By appreciating my buiness and suprising me with an unexpected bonus,
that Etsy seller gave me a memorable customer experience, and won my return business< br/>
