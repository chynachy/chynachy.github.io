  • My Delightful Etsy Seller CX

    Handmade jewelry is one of my favorite things to get from Etsy. I recently purchased a yogi mala necklace made of tigers eye beads and a beautiful brass charm sourced from Ethiopia. The seller's note promised shipping within 2 business days, but I had seen no update for 4 days. Despite feeling frustrated and impatient, I decided not to cancel the order. Rather than writing to ask about the hold up from a place of impatience, I simply chose to give it another day or two before I requested a refund. To my delight, the seller wrote to me shortly after informing me that my necklace had been shipped to arrive the next day. They paid to rush the delivery without me having to ask. My necklace was beautifully packaged with a handwritten card thanking me for my patience and business. In addition to that, they sent me a free pair of earrings and a discount code for my next order. By appreciating my business and surprising me with an unexpected bonus, that Etsy seller gave me a memorable customer experience, and won my return business.

    • How Do I Work With Clients in Basecamp 3?

  • Hey there Jane! Thanks for reaching out to us, we're pumped that you're using Basecamp :) Working with clients in Basecamp 3 is a breeze. The very same tools that you're using with your team can be applied to your clients. From the people page of a project, you'll see the option to invite a client to the project. Add any client contacts you'd like to bring in as well. Once you've done this, the client that you added will be highlighted at the top of the people page for the same project. Keep in mind that you get to choose what clients can see! There's more detailed information about what is visible to the client here*.
